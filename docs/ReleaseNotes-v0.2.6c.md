# BA Portal v0.2.6c – Personalised Navigation Labels

## Changes
- Renamed navigation labels to match the existing "Your Library" convention: Dashboard → Your Dashboard, Training → Your Training, Book a Session → Your Bookings, My Account → Your Account.
- Updated the matching page headings and the Dashboard's Quick Links row to stay consistent with the new nav labels.
- Left action-oriented buttons and cards unchanged (e.g. "Book a Session" / "Book Session" on the Dashboard and Support page, "Book a Training Session" on the Training page) since these describe booking a *new* session, not the page identity.

## Testing
1. Sign in to the Portal and check the sidebar reads: Your Dashboard, Your Library, Your Training, Support, Your Bookings, Your Account.
2. Open each renamed page and confirm its heading matches (Your Training, Your Bookings, Your Account).
3. Confirm the Dashboard's "Book a Session" card and button still say that, not "Your Bookings" (it's prompting a new booking, not showing existing ones).
4. Confirm the Quick Links row on the Dashboard also reads "Your Training" and "Your Account".
