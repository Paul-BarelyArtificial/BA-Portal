# BA Portal v0.2.6d – Library Collections

## Changes
- Within each Library category, items that belong to the same Collection (set in the Console — e.g. every part of a training course) now cluster together under a labelled "📚 Collection Name" sub-heading, instead of just being sorted alphabetically with everything else in that category.
- Each card belonging to a Collection now shows a small "Part of {collection}" tag.
- Items with no Collection set are unaffected — they display individually exactly as before.
- Library search now also matches on Collection name.

## Testing
1. Sign in as a customer who can see at least two items that share a Collection, and one that doesn't, in the same category.
2. Open Your Library and confirm the two related items appear together under a Collection sub-heading, with the "Part of..." tag on each.
3. Confirm the unrelated item appears normally below with no sub-heading or tag.
4. Search for the Collection name and confirm both related items are found.
